About FairRide

FairRide helps you see cab prices from multiple platforms in one place so you can always pick the best option.

Our Mission

Bring transparency and fairness to online cab booking by showing you all prices side by side.

Why We Built This

Different apps show different prices for the same route. FairRide reduces confusion and saves your money.
